About Us

Discover how Accessible Chennai is transforming urban mobility for everyone.

Accessible Chennai is a pioneering platform dedicated to making Chennai's public transportation system inclusive for all. Our app leverages cutting-edge AI visual recognition to identify accessibility barriers in real-time, ensuring safe and seamless navigation. With interactive 3D maps featuring accessibility overlays, users can plan routes with confidence, supported by real-time updates from our vibrant community. We offer voice-guided navigation, screen reader compatibility, and wheelchair-accessible route planning to empower individuals with disabilities, seniors, and parents. Join our mission to create a more inclusive Chennai, where everyone can move freely and independently.

The primary purpose of Accessible Chennai is to provide a tailored experience exclusively for individuals with disabilities, ensuring they have access to a transportation system designed with their unique needs in mind. It stands as a beacon of support, bridging gaps in mobility for those who need it most.

This app is exclusively for disabled persons, offering a safe and supportive environment to enhance their independence. By integrating advanced technology with a dedicated community network, Accessible Chennai empowers users with disabilities to overcome physical barriers, connect with local advocates, and access reliable transportation options tailored to their requirements. Our commitment is to make every journey in Chennai a confident and accessible one for this community.
